About Rick Kanashiro!

Rick S. Kanashiro

Birthday : April 9, 2003

Born in Hackensack, New Jersey and raised in Clifton, New Jersey, Rick S. Kanashiro was born to a family of Peruvian-Japanese immigrants. Rick attended Classical Academy Charter school in Clifton, NJ for his middle school education and Passaic County Technical Institute in Wayne, NJ for his high school education. Currently, Rick is majoring in Web & Information Systems at the New Jersey Institute of Technology in Newark, NJ. Rick started his work with the community in 2018, when he partnered with Clifton City Councilwoman Rosemary L. Pino. Under her council, Rick joined the Clifton Traffic Safety Committee where he was recently re-elected to a 3-year term.
